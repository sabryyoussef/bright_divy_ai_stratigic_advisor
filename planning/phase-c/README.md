# Phase C — RFQ & client delivery (steps 23–34)

**Purpose:** Central place for **templates and runbooks** you can fill, send, and track. The master checklist lives in `planning/solution-build-master-steps.md`.

## How to use this folder

1. Work **in order** where dependencies exist: EOI before full proposal; NDA before FSD with sensitive details; FSD before fixed pricing and UAT scenarios.
2. Replace placeholders: `[COMPANY]`, `[CONTACT]`, `[DATE]`, amounts, and dates.
3. **Legal and commercial** content must be reviewed by your counsel and leadership — these files are not legal advice.
